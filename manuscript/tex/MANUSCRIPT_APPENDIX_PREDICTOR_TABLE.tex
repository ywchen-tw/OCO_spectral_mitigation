\caption{Predictor inventory for the ocean and land bias-correction models.
Continuous features are scaled on the training set only (RobustScaler followed
by StandardScaler); the footprint one-hot columns are appended unscaled, and the
vertical-profile EOF block is standardized by its own scaler. The ocean model
uses 34 continuous features and the land model 45, plus the 8 footprint
one-hots and the shared 14-column profile-EOF/tropopause block (four EOF scores
each for the temperature, water-vapor, and CO$_2$-prior profiles, plus two
tropopause terms). The three groups labelled
with an ablation tag (XCO$_2$ departure, spectral photon path-length, and
cloud/aerosol contamination) are the subgroups dropped in the feature-importance
ablation (\texttt{no\_xco2}, \texttt{no\_spec}, \texttt{no\_contam}); the
profile-EOF block is orthogonal and retained in every set. Spectroscopic
$k$-coefficients are taken from the no-Savitzky--Golay fit.}
